Privacy Policy

Posted: 2016/05/01

Pinco is client-only application to help people show the amazing pictures they love from Instagram on Apple TV.

Information Collection And Use

After users' permission by Instagram Oauth certification, Pinco shows the content of users' Instagram account. Pinco does not collect any personal data of users account as we do not hold a remote server. Pinco would not ask for any right to post, update or delete any contents or settings under users' Instagram account.
